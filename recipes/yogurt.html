Ambrosial Honey Yogurt Parfait


About Recipe

This parfait is a divine breakfast that combines creamy Greek yogurt with the natural sweetness of golden honey and the tartness of pomegranate seeds. Topped with figs and walnuts, this dish is as nourishing as it is elegantâ€”a meal truly fit for the gods.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt (plain or vanilla)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 fresh figs, sliced
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1/4 cup crushed walnuts

Steps On How To Cook Recipe

  1. Spoon a layer of Greek yogurt into a glass or bowl.
  2. Drizzle with honey and add a layer of pomegranate seeds.
  3. Repeat the layers until the glass is full.
  4. Top with sliced figs and a sprinkle of crushed walnuts.
  5. Enjoy your ambrosial honey yogurt parfait!
