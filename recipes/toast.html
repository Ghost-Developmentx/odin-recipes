Olympian Avocado Toast


About Recipe

This luxurious avocado toast combines the creamy texture of smashed avocado with the tangy flavor of feta and a hint of spice from chili flakes. A perfectly poached egg crowns this dish, making it the ultimate power breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown and crispy.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth and creamy.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado on the toasted bread slices.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese and chili flakes on top of the avocado.
  6. Poach the eggs to your desired level of doneness.
  7. Place the poached eggs on top of the avocado toast.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy!
